About us

Rainydays is a norwegian lifestyle brand that specialize in rainwear made for people living an active lifestyle.

main goal has always been to keep our customers dry whatever the weather - so that you can keep doing what you love, whatever the weather.

Pushing the Comfort Zone is our core value here at Rainydays - If you always stay in your comfort zone you will never know what your really capable of.

We strive to offer rainwear that will keep you dry in the most extreme conditions, so you can push your comfort zone, whether that means climbing a mountain, paddling down a stream or going for a walk in the rain.

Rainydays was founded Stavanger in 2010.
